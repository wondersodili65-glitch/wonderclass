‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our services

How do I book an appointment?

You can book an appointment using the form above, calling us at 08039433142, or through our mobile app. You can select your preferred doctor, date, and time.

What are your visiting hours?

Outpatient Clinic: 8 AM - 8 PM, Monday to Saturday. Emergency Department: 24/7/365 days a year.

Do you accept insurance?

Yes, we accept all major insurance providers. Please contact our billing department for details.

How soon can I get test results?

Most results are available within 24 hours. Some urgent tests have results within 2-3 hours.

Can I reschedule or cancel an appointment?

Yes, you can reschedule or cancel up to 24 hours before your appointment. Contact us or use the patient portal.
